GOA

Situated on the west coast of India, the small state of Goa is most famous for its stunning beaches. So, plan a weekend trip and cover all of these natural things to see in Goa in one go!

KERALA

The land of God, Kerala is believed to be a gift of the Arabian Sea. The pride of Kerala are Exotic Beaches, Breathtaking hill Stations, Enchanting waterfalls, Beautiful lagoons, Meandering rivers and Amazing natural scenarios. Kerala land of beauty and paradise on earth, has a rich natural heritage kept green and fertile by a network of nearly 41 rivers, which also create the emerald beauty of its serene backwaters.

ASSAM

Termed as the land of “Blue Hills and Red River” Assam is the gateway to the north eastern states and has been aptly described as the sentinel of Northeast India. Seven Indian States and two countries Bhutan and Bangladesh, surround Assam which is also close to India’s international borders with China and Myanmar.
